Co-Education and a School for All Ages

2026 will see the start of one of the most significant and exciting set of changes in the almost 500-year history of Westminster — the introduction of girls into all years, and a new pre-prep.

  • 2026: Girls and boys will join a brand new pre-prep in Reception year at Westminster Under School
  • 2026: Girls will also join boys at Westminster Under School in Years 3 and 7
  • 2026: Girls will join boys in Westminster School’s Year 9 Fifth Form

To help meet the needs of our changing and expanding Westminster, we have also purchased two significant new buildings.

Registration

  • Registration for boys and girls at 4+ for entry to Westminster Under School in September 2026 is now open.
  • Registration for girls at 7+, and 11+ for entry to Westminster Under School in September 2026 is now open.
  • Registration for girls at 13+ for entry to Westminster School in September 2028 is now open.
