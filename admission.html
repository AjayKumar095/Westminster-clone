Each year Westminster School offers places to boys and girls at both 13+ and 16+ entry, while Westminster Under School admits pupils at the 4+, 7+, 8+ and 11+ entry points. Information regarding 4+, 7+, 8+ and 11+ entry can be found on the Under School’s website.

The pages in this section of the website provide details specifically relating to 13+ and 16+ entry to Westminster School, outlining the application process, registration timelines, eligibility criteria, fees and opportunities for assistance with fees (Scholarships and Bursaries), to ensure that prospective pupils and their parents have everything needed to be able to apply for a place at the School. To support the application process, we have a dedicated and experienced admissions team, who are available to answer any questions applicants may have and who make applying to the School easy and undaunting. Find out when your child is able to apply to join the School and view the registration timeline using the personalised registration age checker below.
